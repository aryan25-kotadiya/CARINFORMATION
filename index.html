Specifications

The Maruti Suzuki Alto 800 is a budget-friendly hatchback ideal for city driving. It comes with a 796 cc, 3-cylinder petrol engine producing 48 PS and 69 Nm, paired with a 5-speed manual gearbox. A CNG variant is also available, offering better mileage. The car reaches a top speed of around 140 km/h and delivers mileage of 22.05 km/l (petrol) and 31.59 km/kg (CNG). Compact in size, the Alto 800 measures 3,445 mm in length with a ground clearance of 160 mm and a 177-liter boot. It features front disc and rear drum brakes, and a MacPherson strut suspension system. Key safety features include dual airbags, ABS with EBD, reverse parking sensors, and a speed alert system. The Alto 800 balances efficiency, safety, and basic comfort, making it a smart option for everyday urban use.
